The Origin of Sahrika

In the heart of Barabanki, near the cultural hub of Lucknow, lies the village of Sahri - home to generations of master weavers who have preserved the ancient art of handloom weaving.

Sahrika was born from a desire to honor this heritage, connecting the skilled artisans of Sahri with appreciators of authentic craftsmanship worldwide.
