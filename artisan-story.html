Geographical Indication Tag Awarded to Our Handloom Craft

In a momentous achievement for our artisan community, our traditional handloom craft has been awarded the prestigious Geographical Indication (GI) tag. This recognition not only protects the unique identity of our craft but also acknowledges the centuries-old techniques and cultural significance of our weaving traditions. The GI tag ensures that only textiles produced in our region using authentic methods can bear this distinguished mark of origin.
