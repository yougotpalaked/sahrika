Frequently Asked Questions

How long are gift cards valid?
Our gift cards are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. The expiration date will be clearly mentioned on the gift card.
Can I use multiple gift cards for a single purchase?
Yes, you can use multiple gift cards for a single purchase. If the total purchase amount exceeds the gift card value, you can pay the remaining amount using other payment methods.
Can I return or exchange a gift card?
Gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash. However, if there's an issue with your gift card, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
How quickly will the digital gift card be delivered?
Digital gift cards are delivered instantly to the recipient's email address. You can also schedule the delivery for a future date if you're planning ahead for a special occasion.
Can I customize the gift card design?
Yes, we offer customization options for both digital and physical gift cards. You can choose from various designs or add a personal touch with a custom message.
