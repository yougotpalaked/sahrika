Our Services

Discover the world of handcrafted textiles through our specialized services designed to connect you with the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship.

Experience Sahrika Beyond Products

At Sahrika, we believe in creating meaningful connections between our artisans and customers. Our services are designed to immerse you in the world of traditional textiles while supporting sustainable craftsmanship.

Workshops

Learn traditional weaving techniques directly from our master artisans. Our workshops offer hands-on experience in creating beautiful textiles using age-old methods.

Learn More

Subscription Box

Receive curated handcrafted textiles delivered to your doorstep every month. Each box tells a story of Indian craftsmanship with exclusive products and artisan stories.
