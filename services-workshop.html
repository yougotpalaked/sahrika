Textile Workshops

Learn traditional weaving techniques directly from our master artisans and immerse yourself in the rich heritage of Indian textiles.

Discover the Art of Handloom Weaving

Our workshops offer a unique opportunity to learn directly from skilled artisans who have been practicing traditional textile techniques for generations. Each workshop is designed to provide hands-on experience while preserving and celebrating India's rich textile heritage.

How Our Workshops Work

Register

Choose your preferred workshop and date, then complete the registration process online or by contacting us directly.

Prepare

We'll send you all the necessary information, including what to bring and what to expect during the workshop.

Join us at our studio where our master artisans will guide you through each step of the textile-making process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to join a workshop?

No prior experience is necessary for our beginner workshops. We welcome participants of all skill levels and provide all the guidance you need to get started.

What materials are included in the workshop fee?

All necessary materials and tools are included in the workshop fee. You'll have access to looms, yarns, dyes, and other supplies needed for your specific workshop.

Can I book a private workshop for a group?

Yes, we offer private workshops for groups, corporate events, and special occasions. Please contact us directly to discuss your requirements and arrange a customized workshop experience.

Where are the workshops held?

Our workshops are held at our studio in [Location]. The exact address and directions will be provided upon registration.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans can change. You can reschedule or cancel your workshop up to 48 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are eligible for a 50% refund or the option to reschedule.
